Privacy Policy

Clock Weather Widget — Effective March 17, 2026

Clock Weather Widget ("the App") is developed by Eduardo Solanas. This policy describes what data the App collects, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

Location Data

The App requests access to your device's location (fine and coarse) solely to retrieve weather information for your area. Location coordinates are sent to third-party weather services (see Section 3) and are stored locally on your device so the widget can refresh without re-requesting your position each time. Your location is never sent to our own servers.

App Preferences

Settings you choose (temperature unit, clock format, theme, language) are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted externally.

Advertising Data

The App displays advertisements provided by third-party ad networks. These networks may collect and use data such as your advertising ID, device type, IP address, and general location to serve relevant ads. Please refer to the ad provider's privacy policy for details:

  • Google AdMob Privacy Policy

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Display current weather conditions and forecasts on your home-screen widget.
  • Show relevant advertisements to support free access to the App.
  • Remember your preferences (units, theme, clock format).

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to any party other than the third-party services described in this policy.

3. Third-Party Services

The App communicates with the following external services:

  • WeatherAPI.com — receives your coordinates to return weather data. Privacy Policy
  • Open-Meteo.com — receives your coordinates for weather data and geocoding. Terms
  • Google Play Services — used to obtain device location. Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — serves advertisements within the App. Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage & Security

All weather data, saved locations, and preferences are stored locally on your device in an encrypted data store and a local database. No account creation is required. We do not operate any backend servers that store your data.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

6. Your Choices

  • Location: You can revoke location permission at any time in your device settings. The App will continue to show weather for your last saved location.
  • Ads personalization: You can opt out of personalized ads in your device's Google settings under "Ads".
  • Data deletion: Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
